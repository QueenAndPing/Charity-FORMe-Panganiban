Three Reasons Why I Support Angat Buhay Foundation

1. Through a collaborative effort with local government units and other stakeholders of different sectors on community assessment initiatives, the Angat Buhay Foundation manages to identify vital interventions needed for poverty alleviation.

2. The foundation has also received recognition for its prompt response during natural disasters - delivering immediate assistance including shelters and food supplies.

3. To guarantee transparency and accountability, Angat Buhay Foundation enables donors to observe the specific purpose of their contributions as these aid those in need. Moreover, the foundation implements a system for complaint resolution that allows beneficiaries to voice out any apprehension or grievances against its activities.
